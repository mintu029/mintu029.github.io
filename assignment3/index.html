About Minh Tu

Le Minh Tu is a young artist who is only 20 years old. He graduated from high school in the specialized printing class of Tien Giang High School for the Gifted. He graduated from Ho Chi Minh Conservatory with a major in guitar. And is currently a final year student majoring in Digital Media at RMIT University. Because of the artistic influence from his family since childhood, he has had many experiences in his own creative process. He has strengths in photography, film, and music.

For inquiries, collaborations, or to learn more about Minh Tu's creative process, feel free to reach out via the contact section.
